Strawberry Rhubarb Pie

This strawberry rhubarb pie is a favorite springtime dessert in our house.
The filling has a sweet-tart flavor; bake in your favorite pie crust and serve with vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  1. Sugar
  2. Flour
  3. Rhubarb
  4. Strawberries
  5. Pie crust
  6. Butter
  7. Egg yolk

Steps

  • Make filling
  • Fill pie
  • Bake!
